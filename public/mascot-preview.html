La Iguana
Patient. Calculated. Caribbean cool.

Visual Direction

Geometric-stylized Caribbean iguana. Upright bipedal stance. Think Shepard Fairey meets Nike SB. Half-lidded amber eyes. Optional thin gold chain or cap.

Three Poses

  • Neutral: Arms crossed, observing
  • Thinking: Hand on chin, tile nearby
  • Celebrating: Fist up, controlled joy

Personality

Patient Calculated Cool Culturally rooted Quietly dominant

Best For

  • Thumbnails (expressions match content)
  • App icon (head + crest only)
  • Merch (t-shirt silhouette, cap patch)
  • Loading screens

Pros

  • Culturally authentic (Caribbean native)
  • Unique — nobody has mascot iguana
  • Adult appeal, not childish
  • Scales from icon to billboard

Cons

  • Needs quality illustration
  • Iguana ≠ domino (not obvious link)
  • Green on dark needs contrast care
👴
El Capo
Timeless. Aspirational. The master's silhouette.

Visual Direction

Cuban poster art meets Saul Bass. Never fully revealed — mystique is the point. Fedora silhouette. Minimal. Aspirational.

Three Variations

  • Full Silhouette: Seated figure, fedora, holding tile
  • Hands Only: Two hands on table with tile
  • Iconic Profile: Minimal line art, fedora star

Personality

Authoritative Timeless Aspirational Minimal Cultural gravitas

Best For

  • Channel banners / YouTube headers
  • About page hero section
  • Premium merch (screenprint)
  • Achievement badge ("El Capo" level)

Pros

  • Timeless — won't look dated
  • Strong emotional pull
  • Works in 2-color for print
  • Sophisticated, editorial

Cons

  • Too cold for beginner content
  • Lacks expressiveness
  • Too abstract for app icon

La Ficha
The game piece IS the mascot.
[1|1] Friendly
[3|3] Focused
[6|6] Intense

Visual Direction

Domino tile geometry becomes the face. Pips ARE the expression system. Coral dividing line = mouth/expression. Stands upright at jaunty angle.

Expression System

  • [1|1]: Wide eyes, smile — beginner
  • [3|3]: Squinted focus — strategy
  • [6|6]: Intense, serious — expert

Personality

Expressive Approachable Witty Scalable Adaptable

Best For

  • App icon (STRONGEST)
  • Difficulty indicators
  • Animated stickers
  • Achievement badges (pip = level)

Pros

  • Most readable at tiny sizes
  • Self-explaining — instant recognition
  • Infinite expression system
  • Lowest risk — geometric, hard to fail

Cons

  • Could feel "too cute"
  • Less "cool factor"
  • Domino tiles are generic symbols
🦎⚡
El Lagarto
Miami Vice meets esports. Sunglasses never come off.

Visual Direction

Aggressive iguana variant. Angular jaw, dramatic crest. Reflective sunglasses ALWAYS on. Optional hoodie. Esports team logo energy.

Three Views

  • Front: Confrontational stance, direct eye contact
  • Profile: Low-angle, dramatic crest reveal
  • Icon: Head + sunglasses only (badge)

Personality

Aggressive Stylish Urban Fierce Magnetic

Best For

  • Tournament/competitive content
  • YouTube thumbnails (aggressive titles)
  • Merch (hoodie print, phone cases)
  • Competitive mode UI face

Pros

  • Strongest merch potential
  • Appeals to 18-30 audience
  • Esports-ready
  • High social shareability

Cons

  • May alienate traditional audience
  • Trend-dependent aesthetic
  • Too aggressive for tutorials

🎯 Luna's Recommendation: Dual System

Use multiple mascots for different emotional registers:

Role Mascot Why
App Icon / UI La Ficha Best at small sizes, brand-connected
Brand Character La Iguana Ownable, culturally authentic
Premium Emblem El Capo Aspirational, editorial quality
Competitive Mode El Lagarto Reserved for tournaments/esports

Together they cover the full player journey: curious → regular → aspirational → competitive
